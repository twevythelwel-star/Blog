About This Blog

Signal / Noise is a five-essay investigation into human-AI relationships, written as a Web Development Fundamentals course project and structured with Jekyll static site generator conventions.

The subject: What happens at the intersection of loneliness and algorithms? Nearly half of Gen Z reports meaningful relationships with AI systems. This blog takes that fact seriously rather than dismissing it, and tries to understand it clearly.

The approach: Each post is a personal investigation, drawing on peer-reviewed research, primary experience, and reported accounts from real people. The goal is honesty over comfort.

The author: A Web Development Fundamentals student at [University Name], Montego Bay, Jamaica. Course: Web Development Fundamentals, June 2026.
