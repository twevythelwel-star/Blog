About This Blog

AI & ME is a five-Post Blog understanding the human-AI relationships, written fora Web Development Fundamentals Assignment and structured with Jekyll static site generator conventions.

The Subject: What happens in between loneliness and algorithms? Nearly half of Gen Z reports meaningful relationships with AI systems. This blog takes that seriously rather than dismissing it.

The Approach: Each post is a personal investigation, drawing on peer-reviewed research, primary experience, and reported accounts from real people. The goal is honesty over comfort.

The Author: A Web Development Fundamentals student at MIU International, Trevaughn Thelwell Jamaica. Course: Web Application Development (BSCS)(PLAN2025) PER13610 MAY26 6SEM June 21 ,2026.
